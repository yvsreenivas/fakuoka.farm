Natural and Organic Farming Resource

Inspired by Japanese farmer Masanobu Fukuoka (1913 – 2008)

Fukuoka farming is a radical, no-till, no herbicide and no pesticide farming method developed by Japanese farmer Masanobu Fukuoka (1913 – 2008). The food products of fukuokan small farm are based on a combination of natural farming and permaculture techniques. This is exemplified by his book; The One-Straw Revolution.

My vision is to create a resource for natural farmers on the internet. When I came across Fukuokan farming method and learned about his philosophy and technique, I was hooked. What an inspiring man! Natural farmers with little resources can easily access the knowledge base that we are creating.
